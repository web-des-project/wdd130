Rivers

Learn About The Patagonia's Rivers

Río "El Manso", Los lagos

The Manso River is a natural water course that rises at the foot of Mount Tronador and flows zigzagging south into Argentine territory and finally crosses the border into Chilean territory where it flows into the Puelo River.

Río Baker, Aysén

The Baker River is a natural water course that originates in Lake Bertrand and flows in a general southerly direction in the Aysén Region of General Carlos Ibáñez del Campo until it flows into the Pacific Ocean.

Río Futaleufú, Los lagos

The Futaleufú River is a fluvial channel, classified as a river, fed by lakes in the Los Alerces National Park in the province of Chubut, Argentina, it crosses the Andes mountain range towards Chile, flowing into Lake Yelcho.
