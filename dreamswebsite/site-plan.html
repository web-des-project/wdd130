Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website it is to encourage people with small bussiness to create their own website so they can sell their products or offer their services. This page must attract people to choose my services and should convince them that we have better options for their necessities than the competitors. The website should generate incomes, costumers and must build trust.

Audience

This website is intended to be for people with small bussiness who have the desire of increase their incomes and to reach more costumers by creating their own website. The costumers that visit this webpage will be over 18 years, they will likely have a growth mindeset so they look to the future with greate expectative. The people who visit this site are looking for someone who can help them to upgrade the marketing and make their small bussines more attractive for potentitial costumers. In this site the costumers will be accesing by their phones or desktops

Content

Home page

If you want to take your business to the next level and earn new customers, this webpage is the best place where you can be. Dream’s website is here to give you the possibility to design your own website for your business. We are here to help you with the development and design of your choice for it. There are many options for you to make the webpage of your dreams, and we bring you the tools to make it. We have professional people working together to bring you the best experience and the best service. The only thing you need to do is give us your idea and you can leave the rest in our hands.

Images for the Home page

Required information

The first thing we need to know is the type of business you are running. We try to make a unique website for every customer and make it very personal for them, that is why we need all the details you may want so we can create a proper website. You can also give us the color palette of your choice, or we can choose one for you to make your website looking good and striking for the customers. Also, we need to know the purpose of your website and the audience. With these ideas in mind, we can accomplish the best results. On other hand, we will always want to create a good and respectful working area, so it is important to always keep these values between the “Dream’s website” team and the customers. If there are any other things you want to know or any questions you have, you can always send us an email; we will be happy to answer and help you to understand better how we work.

Images for the Page 2

Services and prices

The information you were asked to give is especially important for us so we can give you the best prices and services according to your needs. Dream’s website offers many options and different accessible prices for every customer. The base price for the website's creation is $40.00, including the base design and color palette. The price will be higher depending on how many things and details your site will have, like the number of images and pages or how many transitions or animations you want for your website. If you want to add any other thing, you can specify it and we will set up the price. We may have a logo for your business, but if you do not have one, we can also create a special one for you, which will match the design and style of your website, always respecting your principal ideas and the color palette. We have two different prices for this service. The basic one consists in a logo made only with vectors and one or two specific colors; the price of this one is $20.00. The other one has more details (according to the customer instructions); this logo can have vectors, as many colors as you want, or it can be a drawing made 100% by us. This one has a price of $45.00. All the logos include the text area where the business name will go.
