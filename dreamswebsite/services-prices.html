Services and Prices

Prices

The information you were asked to give is especially important for us so we can give you the best prices and services according to your needs. Dream's website offers many options and different accessible prices for every customer. The base price for the website's creation is $40.00, including the base design and color palette. The price will be higher depending on how many things and details your site will have, like the number of images and pages or how many transitions or animations you want for your website. If you want to add any other thing, you can specify it and we will set up the price.

Logo prices

You may have a logo for your business, but if you do not have one, we can also create a special one for you, which will match the design and style of your website, always respecting your principal ideas and the color palette. We have two different prices for this service. The basic one consists in a logo made only with vectors and one or two specific colors; the price of this one is $20.00. The other one has more details (according to the customer instructions); this logo can have vectors, as many colors as you want, or it can be a drawing made 100% by us. This one has a price of $45.00. All the logos include the text area where the business name will go.
