Required Information

What do we need?

The first thing we need to know is the type of business you are running. We try to make a unique website for every customer and make it very personal for them, that is why we need all the details you may want so we can create a proper website. You can also give us the color palette of your choice, or we can choose one for you to make your website looking good and striking for the customers. Also, we need to know the purpose of your website and the audience.

With these ideas in mind, we can accomplish the best results. On other hand, we will always want to create a good and respectful working area, so it is important to always keep these values between the “Dream’s website” team and the customers. If there are any other things you want to know or any questions you have, you can always send us an email; we will be happy to answer and help you to understand better how we work.
